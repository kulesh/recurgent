> # Jaffna Kool Recipe

Jaffna Kool is a traditional Sri Lankan refreshing drink/porridge from the Jaffna region, typically served cold. Here's a good recipe:

## Ingredients:

**For the Kool base:**
- 1 cup palmyra root flour (or substitute with a mix of rice flour and ragi/finger millet flour)
- 8-10 cups water
- Salt to taste

**For the accompaniments:**
- 1 cup fresh coconut, finely shredded
- 2-3 shallots, finely chopped
- 2 green chilies, finely chopped
- Fresh lime juice (from 2-3 limes)
- Jaggery or palm sugar (optional, for sweetness)
- Ice cubes

**Optional additions:**
- Cooked palmyra tubers (cut into small pieces)
- Cooked green gram (mung beans)
- Fresh ginger, grated

## Instructions:

1. **Prepare the flour mixture:**
   - Mix the palmyra root flour (or flour substitute) with 2 cups of cold water to form a smooth paste without lumps.

2. **Cook the kool:**
   - Bring remaining 6-8 cups of water to a boil in a large pot.
   - Slowly add the flour paste while stirring continuously to prevent lumps.
   - Add salt to taste.
   - Reduce heat and simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally until the mixture thickens to a porridge-like consistency.
   - Remove from heat and let it cool completely.

3. **Chill:**
   - Once cooled, refrigerate for at least 2-3 hours or until thoroughly chilled.

4. **Serve:**
   - Pour the chilled kool into serving bowls.
   - Add shredded coconut, chopped shallots, green chilies, and any optional additions.
   - Squeeze fresh lime juice over the top.
   - Add ice cubes for extra refreshment.
   - If desired, add a bit of jaggery for sweetness (though traditionally it's more savory).

## Tips:
- Authentic Jaffna Kool uses palmyra root flour, which gives it a distinctive flavor and texture.
- The consistency should be like a thin porridge - not too thick, not too watery.
- Adjust the spice level by varying the amount of green chilies.
- This is best served ice-cold, especially on hot days.
- Some variations include adding seafood like dried fish or prawns.

Enjoy this traditional Jaffna specialty!
